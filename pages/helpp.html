Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sign up for an account?
    To sign up for an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions to create your account.
  • How do I reset my password?
    If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions.
  • How do I search for courses?
    You can search for courses by entering keywords in the search bar located at the top of the page. You can also browse courses by category.
  • How do I contact support?
